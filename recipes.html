Healthy Breakfast Recipes

1🍇 Greek Yogurt Berry Bowl

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup mixed berries
  • 2 tbsp granola
  • 1 tsp chia seeds
  • 1 tsp honey (optional)

Instructions

  1. Add the Greek yogurt to a bowl.
  2. Top with mixed berries, granola, and chia seeds.
  3. Drizzle with honey if desired.
  4. Serve immediately.
2🥑 Avocado Toast with Egg

Ingredients

  • 2 slices whole-grain bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 eggs
  • Salt and black pepper
  • Chili flakes (optional)

Instructions

  1. Toast the bread.
  2. Mash the avocado and spread it on the toast.
  3. Cook the eggs to your preference.
  4. Place the eggs on top of the avocado.
  5. Season with salt, pepper, and chili flakes.
3🍌 Banana Oat Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • Cinnamon to taste

Instructions

  1. Blend all ingredients until smooth.
  2. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat.
  3. Pour small portions of batter onto the pan.
  4. Cook for 2–3 minutes per side.
  5. Serve with fresh fruit.
4🥬 Spinach Omelette

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup fresh spinach
  • 1/4 cup low-fat cheese
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs with salt and pepper.
  2. Cook the spinach in a pan for 1 minute.
  3. Pour in the eggs.
  4. Sprinkle cheese on top.
  5. Fold the omelette and serve.

Healthy Lunch Recipes

1🍗 Grilled Chicken Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 grilled chicken breast
  • Mixed salad greens
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Cucumber
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Slice the grilled chicken.
  2. Combine vegetables in a bowl.
  3. Add the chicken.
  4. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice.
  5. Toss and serve.
2🍚 Turkey and Rice Bowl

Ingredients

  • 200 g ground turkey
  • 1 cup cooked brown rice
  • Broccoli
  • Carrots
  • Soy sauce

Instructions

  1. Cook the turkey until browned.
  2. Steam the vegetables.
  3. Add rice to a bowl.
  4. Top with turkey and vegetables.
  5. Drizzle with soy sauce.
3🌾 Quinoa Vegetable Bowl

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • Bell pepper
  • Zucchini
  • Chickpeas
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Roast the vegetables.
  2. Combine quinoa and chickpeas.
  3. Add roasted vegetables.
  4. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice.
  5. Mix well before serving.
4🐟 Baked Salmon with Sweet Potato

Ingredients

  • 1 salmon fillet
  • 1 sweet potato
  • Green beans
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic powder

Instructions

  1. Bake the salmon at 200°C (400°F) for 15–18 minutes.
  2. Roast the sweet potato.
  3. Steam the green beans.
  4. Serve everything together.

Healthy Dinner Recipes

1🥢 Chicken Stir-Fry

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Broccoli
  • Bell peppers
  • Carrots
  • Garlic
  • Low-sodium soy sauce

Instructions

  1. Cook the chicken until golden.
  2. Add garlic and vegetables.
  3. Stir-fry for 5–7 minutes.
  4. Add soy sauce.
  5. Serve hot.
2🍝 Turkey Meatballs with Vegetables

Ingredients

  • Ground turkey
  • Egg
  • Oats
  • Zucchini noodles
  • Tomato sauce

Instructions

  1. Mix turkey, egg, and oats.
  2. Form meatballs.
  3. Bake for 20 minutes.
  4. Heat the tomato sauce.
  5. Serve over zucchini noodles.
3🍤 Shrimp and Vegetable Skillet

Ingredients

  • Shrimp
  • Broccoli
  • Bell pepper
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Cook garlic in olive oil.
  2. Add shrimp and cook for 3 minutes.
  3. Add vegetables.
  4. Finish with lemon juice.
  5. Serve immediately.
4🫑 Stuffed Bell Peppers

Ingredients

  • 2 bell peppers
  • Lean ground beef or turkey
  • Brown rice
  • Tomato sauce
  • Low-fat cheese

Instructions

  1. Cook the meat and rice.
  2. Mix with tomato sauce.
  3. Fill the peppers.
  4. Top with cheese.
  5. Bake for 30 minutes at 190°C (375°F).
